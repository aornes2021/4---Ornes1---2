Product Details

Featuring the latest in aromatherapy technology, the Viva Naturals Ultrasonic Diffuser uses less oil than other diffusers, vaporizing without the use of heat to maximize the benefits of your favorite essential oils. Savor their calming or energizing properties as the delicate mist fills your room with pure, therapeutic delight. Complimented by a selection of built-in LED lights to deepen the sensory experience, the Viva Naturals Ultrasonic Diffuser makes it easy to incorporate the benefits of aromatherapy into your everyday life.
